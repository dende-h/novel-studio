第四章

The rain fell steadily against the glass, blurring the neon lights of the city below into streaks of crimson and indigo. He sat at the heavy oak desk, staring at the blank sheet of paper before him.

It was time to write the final chapter.

The 運命うんめい that had bound them together was fraying. She had left only a single word inscribed on the mirror: 忘却ぼうきゃく.

He picked up the fountain pen, feeling its cool weight. "I will not forget," he whispered to the empty room. "Even if the 世界せかい demands it."
